To create a proper favicon:

1. Create a square version of your logo (ideally 32x32, 64x64, or 128x128 pixels)
2. Convert it to ICO format
3. Name it "favicon.ico"
4. Place it in the public folder

You can use online tools like:
- favicon.io
- realfavicongenerator.net
- faviconer.com
